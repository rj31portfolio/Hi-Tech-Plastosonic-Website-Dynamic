Businesses

Chemical

Hi-Tech Plastosonic Chemical Business delivers world-class solutions in the field of chemicals and allied industries. It offers expertise across project design, engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning, serving sectors such as petrochemicals, fertilizers, and specialty chemicals with a strong focus on innovation and sustainability.

Engineering

HI-Tech Plasosonic specializes in advanced engineering solutions with a focus on precision, innovation, and quality. Our expertise covers design, development, and manufacturing of high-performance systems tailored to meet industrial needs across multiple sectors.

Electric Vehicle

Hi-Tech Plasosonic Electric Vehicle Solutions focuses on sustainable mobility by providing end-to-end engineering, design, and deployment of electric vehicle technologies. Our solutions include EV charging infrastructure, battery systems, powertrain development, and smart energy management for a cleaner and greener transportation ecosystem.

Minerals & Metals

The business undertakes EPC projects for ferrous and non-ferrous metal industries, as well as bulk material and ash handling systems in the power, port, steel and mining sectors.

Heavy Engineering

L&T Heavy Engineering manufactures and supplies custom designed equipment & critical piping to process industries such as fertilizer, chemical, refinery, petrochemical, and oil & gas, as well as to sectors such as thermal & nuclear power and aerospace.

Precision Engineering and Systems

Shipbuilding

Construction & Mining Machinery

L&T Valves

L&T Valves is a leader in flow-control solutions for critical services. The business has a marketing network that spans the globe and is reinforced through strategic alliances with international distributors.

LTIMindtree

L&T Technology Services

L&T Technology Services offers design and development solutions through the entire development chain in multiple industries. The Company has design and delivery locations in six centres in India, as well as two delivery centres in the US.

L&T Metro Rail

The Hyderabad Metro Rail is the world’s largest metro project in public-private partnership mode. An iconic urban mass rapid transit system of Hyderabad, it covers 69.2 km across three corridors, transforming the way city travels, promoting sustainability, triggering robust economic activity in the region.

L&T Finance

L&T Finance (LTF) is one of the leading, retail oriented, Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs). It offers a wide range of financial products and services catered to the urban & rural audiences through its wholly-owned subsidiaries.

L&T Realty

The real estate arm of L&T extends the Company’s values of trust, professionalism and commitment to the domain of commercial and residential properties. All properties of L&T Realty embody a sustainable design philosophy and focus on fostering integrated communities.

Read more

GreenTech

L&T’s Green Manufacturing and Development business is at the forefront of the global shift towards a greener future. The business encompasses the Green Energy Value Chain – Development, Manufacturing and EPC.

Read more

L&T EduTech

L&T EduTech enables globally employable, industry-ready talent through state-of-the-art, application-oriented learning, skilling, assessment and certification programs, powered by L&T's decades-long expertise in engineering and technology. It partners with universities, corporates, NGOs and government to expand access to world-class technical and skill driven education.

L&T-Cloudfiniti

L&T-Cloudfiniti is driving India’s digital transformation with innovative solutions in Data Centers, Multi-Cloud, Managed Services, Network Security, and Application Integration. Our AI-ready Hyperscale Data Centers in Mumbai and Chennai are engineered for scalability, delivering seamless performance and unmatched reliability to meet evolving enterprise demands.

L&T Semiconductor Technologies (LTSCT)

L&T Semiconductor Technologies (LTSCT), is a global semiconductor product company. LTSCT is building a semiconductor portfolio of smart devices across MEMS Sensors, Power, Analog Mixed Signal & RF products, Compute and modules to support Mobility, Industrial, and Energy applications.

L&T-SuFin is an integrated platform for buying and selling of industrial products and services. Combining Financing and Logistics support on the same platform, it enables businesses, especially MSMEs, to source their industrial suppliers pan-India, digitally and cost-effectively.
